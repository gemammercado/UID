Primary Colors

Primary colors are the foundation of all other colors. These are red, blue, and yellow. They cannot be made by mixing other colors.

Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colors. These include green (blue + yellow), orange (red + yellow), and purple (red + blue).

Tertiary Colors

Tertiary colors are made by mixing a primary color with a neighboring secondary color. Examples include red-orange, yellow-green, and blue-purple.
